# 🕵️ Operation: Red Thread - Rounds & Rules

## 🎯 Mission Objective
Investigate the suspicious death of investigative journalist **Priya Singh**. Local authorities have closed the case as a natural cardiac event, but intelligence suggests foul play involving **Sundaram Infrastructure**.

**Primary Goal:** Breach the network, recover deleted evidence, and piece together the final 11 minutes leading to her death.

## 📜 Rules of Engagement
1.  **Asymmetric Visibility:** You do **not** share the same screen capabilities. One operative will see solutions or threats the other cannot.
2.  **Access Denied:** If an interface shows `ACCESS DENIED`, your partner must execute the task.
3.  **Mandatory Communication:** Verbal communication is required for survival and puzzle solving.
4.  **Terminal Overrides:** Intercept hidden override codes (e.g., `DEFUSE-XXXX`) and enter them into the global command terminal to neutralize security.
